Training Guidelines

Are you frustrated ? Dealing with your pets behaviours ? Not coming back when called? Struggling with leash pulling?

We got you !

Pet training and socialization are among the most essential aspects of a responsible pet owner. Moreover proper exercise,necessary nutrient intake is important as well. If you find yourself constantly sruggling with undesirable behaviours and unhealthy patterns of your pet, it is vital to consult guidance and support from a well experienced staff. Well-behaved and socialized pets are more happier, more confident and shows minimum risk. No matter what breed or their age, our staff is comfortable with any circumstance. Our staff along with the training instructor makes path step by step as it is more easier for the pet to get used to the schedule. The owner can decide what behaviours to be trained and the performances which need to be improved.You can make arrangements with your schedules and we are flexible with your choice.

What we provide

Manners and Obedience

An obedient and a well-behaving characteristics of a pet can strengthen thr bond between owner and the pet. One of the most important training regiments begins with potty-training,as the pet is trained with manners of behaviour. Simple behaviours like sit,down,stay,leave and come can be used as the initial step of developing the obedience of a pet. The owner can decide to which extent their pet should be trained and we are responsible from there onwards.

Performance

A little extra training beyond day to day activities. If you want your pet to be trained for a competition or may be as a performer, your pet is in the perfect hands. We are equiped with a well-experienced staff and instructors and we will mould your pet's performances exactly the way you expect. We have a systematic methodology so as to make the pet coperate with us efficiently. Stritly no harm is done in any of our training sessions and the safety of the pet is considered as our main priority.
